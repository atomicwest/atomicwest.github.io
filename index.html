VR Pac-Man Clone



I am currently working on a VR version of Pac-Man on Unity. The basic gameplay is done:

  • PacMan moves in the direction you are facing
  • Consuming pellets increases your score
  • Consuming super pellets makes ghosts edible for a short time
  • Touching a ghost otherwise results in a loss of a life
  • Basic fidgeting animations are provided to ghosts
  • The game ends when you are touched by a ghost and have no lives
  • Ghosts have a generic wandering script and aimlessly move about the maze




The next goal is to program a proper pathfinding algorithm for the ghosts that will hopefully will mimic the behavior in the original game. Github is here.
