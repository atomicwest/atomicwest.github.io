Motion Tracking with 2 Smartphone Cameras

A video posted by Atomik Jëssõn West Gohh (@atomicwest_og) on Feb 28, 2016 at 8:00pm PST

I tried using MotionBuilder to capture convincing track points and realistic animation for character rigs in Maya. With some tape, I marked the major joints on my left hand/fingers and created a crosshair with a Sharpie for improved visibility during automatic tracking. One old Galaxy was propped above my hand while I held up another Galaxy to record at another angle. I just twitched my fingers for a minute and imported the footage as a .png sequence into MotionBuilder Unfortunately, it was hard to resolve cases where the marker disappears from view (i.e. making a fist) and I didn't mark good trackers to establish a consistent coordinate system. I used the hand skeleton model, but at the time it was better to parent the IK handles to the imported trackers. In the future, I think MotionBuilder would be good if the live-action subject had trackers that corresponded to something like the IK handles or rig controllers on the models I use.
