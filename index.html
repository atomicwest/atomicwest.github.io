VR Pac-Man

I created a VR version of Pac-Man on Unity with character models created in Blender. The Github page is here and beta releases are here. Some of the gameplay features include:

  • PacMan moves in the direction you are facing
  • Consuming pellets increases your score
  • Consuming super pellets makes ghosts edible for a short time
  • Touching a ghost otherwise results in a loss of a life
  • Basic fidgeting animations are provided to ghosts
  • The game ends when you are touched by a ghost and have no lives
  • Ghosts normally chase PacMan but run away when a super pellet has been eaten.
  • Sound effects and cues have been added
  • UI elements such as score counter, timer, and mini-map appear
  • Introduction sequence has been added
  • Ability to restart or continue to next level


A long term goal will be to add a hall of fame on a device or on a web service.

Dragon Dagger/Brass Instrument Simulator

Available on Google Play, and the Github is here.

Android Studio project that maps specific musical notes to different buttons of the stylized dragon dagger/flute, as used by the Green Ranger in Mighty Morphin' Power Rangers from the 1990's. There are two mapping modes that can be switched by pressing the center power coin. The first mapping currently supports 4 notes while the second mapping supports 3 chords.
