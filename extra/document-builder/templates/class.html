Certain items, skills, attribute bonuses, and action penalties depend on the class that you choose for your character. Specialized spellcasters (Mages, Warlocks) have access to nearly all spells in the compendium arcana but are unable to use more advanced armor and weaponry that would be accessible to physical combat-focused classes (Rogues, Barbarians). Classes that can balance physical and non-physical aspects (Paladins, Bards) have limited access to both skill sets.
